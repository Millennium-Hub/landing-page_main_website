Tech workshop for Deaf Adults in Koforidua

The Tech Workshop for Deaf Adults in Koforidua was a transformative event that aimed to empower and equip participants with essential digital skills. In a supportive and inclusive environment, deaf adults had the opportunity to learn about various technologies, including web development, basic coding, and drone piloting. Led by dedicated instructors and supported by sign language interpreters, the workshop provided a unique platform for deaf adults to explore the world of technology and break barriers. With a focus on accessibility, the workshop opened doors to new opportunities and unleashed the potential of the participants. It was a heart-warming celebration of inclusivity, where the power of technology transcended boundaries and created a pathway for growth and success.
